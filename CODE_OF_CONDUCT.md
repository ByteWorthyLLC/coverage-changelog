# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Keep discussions focused on the project, the public CMS data, and downstream operator utility.
- Assume practical RCM and policy-monitoring deployments require care around accuracy, neutrality, and source citation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private payer data, claims data, contractor credentials, or PHI in public issues.
- Pressuring maintainers to add telemetry, paid API dependencies, clinical interpretation, or regulatory advice.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, close, or remove issues, pull requests, or comments that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from the repository.

## Reporting

Email conduct concerns to `conduct@byteworthy.io`.
